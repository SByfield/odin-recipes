True Blue Fin Saute

Elephant and Pacific Blue Fin Tuna Sauté

One of Chef Sanji's signature dishes. A elegant dish which he crafted during his time at the Baratie. Although Chef Zeff pretended he did not approve, in secret he was elated at the progress and skills of his young apprentice.

"The All Blue is Real!"

Ingredients

    Serves 4
    • 4 pieces (300g) blue fin tuna
    • dash salt
    • dash white pepper
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    Sauce
    • ½ (100g) onion
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • ½ - 1 tsp grated garlic
    • 2 tbsp water
    • 2 tsp honey
    • 2/3 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp soy sauce
    • 3 tbsp roasted white sesame

Directions

  1. Season tuna with salt and white pepper. Finely slice the onion for the sauce. Put olive oil in a heated frying pan and cook both sides of the tuna and take out of the pan.
  2. Make the sauce. Place olive oil in the frying pan used to cook the tuna. Add the onion and stir fry until transparent. Then add garlic, water, honey, salt, white pepper and white sesame.
  3. Plate fish and top with sauce.
